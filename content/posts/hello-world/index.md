Every blog starts somewhere. This is mine.

I'm Suyan — a software engineer in Seattle. I spend my days building large-scale backend systems at Amazon, and my nights thinking about what else I want to build. This site is where I'll write about that: side projects, things I've learned, ideas I'm turning over.

No particular schedule. No particular audience. Just a place to think out loud.

---

A few things I'm currently interested in:

- High-performance systems and what it takes to build them well
- The intersection of finance and technology — quant, fintech, trading infrastructure
- Tools that reduce digital noise rather than add to it
- Learning Rust properly, not just dabbling

If any of that overlaps with what you're curious about, stick around.
